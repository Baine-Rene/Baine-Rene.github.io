About Me

I am a 19-year-old recent graduate of the IB diploma program, and I'm excited to invite you into my world of creativity and design. Having set foot into the realm of UI design, I'm dedicated to learning and growing with every pixel I place. My ambition is to collaborate with forward-thinking brands, startups, and businesses, helping them bridge the gap between creativity and functionality.


I'm excited to embrace innovation and deliver designs that stand the test of time. By combining my academic foundation, my passion for design, and my insatiable desire to learn, I'm committed to creating interfaces that not only look beautiful but also resonate with users.
